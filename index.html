    • Our Mission

    We are committed to helping improve the environment through reducing Lee High School's environmental impact. We're starting this initiative at LMHS by creating a recycling program and hosting awareness events throughout the school year, in cooperation with Lee High's Eco Club.

    Why Recycle?

    Waste has a huge negative impact on the environment and recycling helps to reduce this impact. Waste:

  • contributes to global warming
  • harms wild animals and the natural environment
  • produces methane gas
  • and much more

    • In the US, Louisiana ranks:

  • 47th, environmental quality
  • 48th, energy consumption
  • 50th, eco-friendly behavior
